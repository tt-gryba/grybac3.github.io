--- layout: default title: Identifying Problematic Trading Patterns ---

TT Score analyzes data for the following problematic trading patterns:

  • Spoofing: uses machine learning technology to identify various patterns of manipulative or disruptive trading activity that involves placing a number of orders a trader has no intention of executing in an attempt to move the market.
  • Abusive Messaging: identifies quote stuffing schemes designed to introduce predictable latency into an exchange's quoting engine or malfunctioning algorithms that might cause market disruptions.
  • Wash Trading (Trader ID): identifies when the same trader ID is both the buyer and seller in the same transaction.
  • Wash Trading (Account): identifies when the same account is both the buyer and seller in the same transaction.
  • Momentum Ignition: searches for behaviors that indicate an attempt to create an artifiicial price movement with aggressive orders followed by an attempt to capitalize on such movement.
  • Pinging: identifies the entry of multiple small orders intended to discover hidden book depth followed by a series of order actions designed to force the large order to trade at less desirable prices.
